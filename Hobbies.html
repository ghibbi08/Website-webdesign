Hobbies

Hello and welcome to my hobby section! As you can see, I have only one hobby: crocheting. When I see something that I want to attempt as a hobby, such as needlework, scrapbooking, athletics, or playing an instrument, I tend to become bored of it after a while, so when I first chose to start crocheting, I was nervous because what if I got bored after a week? I just wasted my time and money. But I'm happy I didn't grow bored easily, and I get to create items I wanted to have give away to people I care about, as well as sell some of them. I hope to add more hobbies in the future!
